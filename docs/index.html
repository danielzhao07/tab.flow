See every tab at a glance

Press Alt+Q and a full-screen overlay shows all your open tabs in a beautiful grid — like Windows Alt+Tab, but for your browser. Thumbnails, favicons, and group colors at a glance.

Start typing and fuzzy search instantly filters your tabs by title or URL. Use powerful structured filters like is:pinned, domain:github.com, or group:work to drill down fast.
